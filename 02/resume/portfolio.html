Jeffrey Wisard

Experience

I've been creating bicycle events and rides since 2010. It started with the Atlanta BeltLine bicycle tour, where I helped take hundreds of bikers around the future BeltLine. From there, I created The Mobile Social - A monthly, slow-roll ride around the city. We'd get 150-200 people each ride. With both experiences under my jersey, I created The Atlanta Cycling Festival - a week long bicycle festival every where people can come and experience life on two wheels.
